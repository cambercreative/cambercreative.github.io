This is the lil tech news you need to know for the week commencing Feb 25, 2021

5 Million Davids vs. Goliath: Major hedge funds got rocked with losses last week after a 5.1 million member Reddit community led a surge in stock purchases of struggling video game retailer, GameStop. The collective buying power was enough to drive the heavily shorted stock up ~2,300% from its more stable early January price.


Our Take

We think this was a great example of how not to introduce price increases and how to act quickly on user feedback to keep a fan base fiercely loyal.
